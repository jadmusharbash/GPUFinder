Did you know that the first GPU (GeForce 256) was made in 1999 by NVIDIA?

Geforce 256

  • The first model in Nvidia's "GeForce" product series was the GeForce 256.
  • It was the first completely Direct3D 7-compliant 3D accelerator and provided a noticeably significant improvement in 3D PC gaming performance.
  • TSMC produced the chip using its 220 nm CMOS technology.
